Hyderabad:

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, blends royal heritage with modernity, offering a unique travel experience. The city is famous for the iconic Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the grand Chowmohallah Palace. It is also known as the "City of Pearls" and is a hub for IT and business. The world-famous Hyderabadi Biryani and bustling markets add to its charm. Whether exploring its Mughal-era architecture or modern attractions, Hyderabad is a city of contrasts.

About:

Hyderabad, the "City of Pearls," is a perfect mix of Mughal heritage and modernity. Tourists visit for its iconic Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the world-famous Hyderabadi Biryani.

Price Range:

Luxury: 5-star hotels (₹6,000+ per night).

Best Places to Stay:
