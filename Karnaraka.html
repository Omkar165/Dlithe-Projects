Welcome to Karnataka

Karnataka:

Karnataka, located in southern India, is known for its rich heritage, stunning landscapes, and vibrant cities. The state is home to the historical ruins of Hampi, the majestic Mysore Palace, and the IT hub of Bengaluru. It boasts scenic hill stations like Coorg and Chikmagalur, along with wildlife sanctuaries like Bandipur. Karnataka’s cuisine, festivals, and diverse culture make it a must-visit destination.

About:

Karnataka offers a blend of heritage, nature, and modern attractions. From the ruins of Hampi and the palaces of Mysore to the coffee plantations of Coorg and the IT hub of Bengaluru, the state has something for every traveler.

Price Range:

Budget travel can start at ₹800–₹1,500 per day, mid-range at ₹2,500–₹5,000, and luxury stays can go beyond ₹7,000 per day.

Accommodation & Stay:

Budget: Hostels, budget hotels (₹500–₹1,500 per night).

Mid-range: 3-star hotels (₹2,000–₹4,000 per night).

Luxury: 5-star hotels (₹6,000+ per night).

Best Places to Stay:

Bengaluru, Mysore, Coorg, Hampi.
