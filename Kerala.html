Welcome to Kerala !

Kerala:

Kerala, often called "God’s Own Country," is famous for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and pristine beaches. The state offers breathtaking hill stations like Munnar, Ayurvedic wellness retreats, and exotic wildlife in Periyar. Houseboat cruises in Alleppey and the vibrant culture of Kochi attract travelers worldwide. Kerala’s rich traditions, Kathakali dance, and delicious cuisine make it a paradise for explorers.

About:

Known as "God’s Own Country," Kerala is famous for its backwaters, lush greenery, and Ayurvedic retreats. From houseboats in Alleppey to the hill stations of Munnar, it is a paradise for nature lovers.

Price Range:

Budget travel can start at ₹800–₹1,500 per day, mid-range at ₹2,500–₹5,000, and luxury stays can go beyond ₹7,000 per day.

Accommodation & Stay:

Budget: Hostels, budget hotels (₹500–₹1,500 per night).

Mid-range: 3-star hotels (₹2,000–₹4,000 per night).

Luxury: 5-star hotels (₹6,000+ per night).

Best Places to Stay:

Kochi, Munnar, Alleppey (houseboats), Kovalam.
